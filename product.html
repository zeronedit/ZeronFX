    About This Product

    Our Cinematic Transitions Pack contains 20 professionally designed transitions that will take your videos to the next level. Each transition has been carefully crafted to provide smooth, eye-catching effects that work perfectly for YouTube videos, commercials, wedding films, and more.

    The pack includes a variety of transition styles including wipes, zooms, spins, light leaks, and distortion effects. All transitions are fully customizable - you can adjust the colors, timing, and intensity to match your project's style.

    How to Use This Product

    Step 1: Install the Transitions

    After downloading, unzip the package and copy the .mogrt files to your Motion Graphics Templates folder:

    Documents/Adobe/Premiere Pro/[version]/Motion Graphics Templates

    Step 2: Access in Premiere Pro

    In Premiere Pro, open the Essential Graphics panel (Window > Essential Graphics). Your transitions will appear under "YourBrand > Cinematic Transitions".

    Step 3: Apply to Your Timeline

    Drag and drop any transition between two clips on your timeline. Adjust the duration by dragging the edges of the transition.

    Step 4: Customize

    Use the Essential Graphics panel to customize colors, timing, and other parameters to match your project's style.
